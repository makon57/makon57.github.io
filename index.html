Intro

I chose to solve bugs each day instead of being a role model for the next generation of change-makers. Aside from living and breathing JavaScript, React, HTML/CSS, Redux, PostgreSQL, Python, SQLAlchemy, and Flask, you can find me reading books or writing my own stories. I'm an avid and competitive life-long learner. I love seeing the people around me grow and I applaud a community that pushes towards growth and encourages intentionality. Work

Bestie is an animal adoption site created with a ReactJS frontend, Python and SQLAlchemy backend and an AWS S3 library.

  • Implemented AWS to allow local image uploads reducing server load time while allowing image scalability of image services.
  • Designed use-interface that provides easier accessibility and more pleasant visuals by implementing efficient and complex designs via ReactJS and CSS3 implementing conditional rendering of features based on the type of user logged in.
  • Ensured user security by implementing frontend and backend authentications through React Router and Flask forms, only allowing logged in users access to particular features depending upon what type of user they are.

SpudHub is an online farmerâ€™s market application created with a ReactJS frontend and Javascript and PostgreSQL backend.

  • Incorporated shopping cart state with localStorage to maintain and persist the user's shopping cart data even after the site refreshed and the user logged out.
  • Constructed a dynamic frontend with ReactJS and CSS3 to provide users with more accessibility to their shopping cart through a sliding cart menu and dynamic buttons for simpler edit and delete functionality.
  • Implemented modals for better access to product information and for a more responsive frontend.

SoulShot is a web application made for photographers to share the stories behind the photos they take.

  • Designed a clean UI/UX using ReactJS to provide a better user experience through hover effect visuals and summaries, and easily accessible functioning and dynamic buttons.
  • Incorporated Express on the backend to manage queries of image albums and used PostgreSQL to maintain and organize the database of images and albums.

About

Hello! My name is Manna Kong and I am a software engineering student at App Academy currently based in Minnesota. I graduated with my Bachelor of Science in English, with minors in Humanities and Writing from the University of Minnesota, Crookston. A fun fact about me is that I lived in South Korea as an English teacher and taught students from kindergarten to sixth grade. I loved each moment of it but still eventually decided teaching wasn't for me, hence the career change into tech.

I enrolled at App Academy in April 2021 and am loving each and every moment here. I'm glad to be a representation of BIPOC in the tech indrustry and hope to continue to contribute to the tech community as an Asian-American woman. My goals after graduation are to build meaningful projects and to work in a team that continously challenges and teaches me how to become better.
